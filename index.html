❄️ Help Protect Internet Freedom

By running a Tor bridge, you can help people access the free and open internet in regions where it's restricted.

It's really simple. You can do it with your home computer. No server needed. Simply open this switch and you can help a person in a censored country access the internet.

Run a Snowflake Proxy

This is the easiest way to help. Just keep this page open.

You will be notified once a person in a censored country gets connected.

Why Run a Bridge?

  • Help people bypass internet censorship
  • Support privacy and freedom of information
  • Contribute to a more open internet

This is a non-profit initiative. We are not affiliated with the Tor Project but support their mission of protecting internet freedom and privacy.
